Dark/Light Theme Toggle Demo

This page demonstrates a simple dark/light theme toggle. Click the button in the top right corner to switch between themes. Your preference will be saved for your next visit.

Customizable Themes

Easily switch between light and dark themes with a single click. The transition is smooth and visually pleasing.

Each theme uses CSS custom properties (variables) for consistent color application across the entire page.

Preferences Saved

Your theme preference is saved in your browser's local storage. When you return, the site will automatically apply your preferred theme.

This provides a personalized experience without requiring an account or login.

Eye Comfort

Dark theme reduces eye strain in low-light environments and can help conserve battery life on OLED screens.

Light theme offers better readability in well-lit environments and is familiar to most users.
